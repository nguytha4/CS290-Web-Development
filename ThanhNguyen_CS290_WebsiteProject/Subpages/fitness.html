Fitness


I'm by no means an expert on the topic of fitness, but I love constantly trying new routines to see what works and what doesn't for my current goals.

Currently, with my current busy schedule, I'm trying the Push/Pull/Legs routine. To quickly explain:

  • The “push” workout trains all of the upper body muscles that are involved in pushing exercises such as the chest, shoulders and triceps.
  • The “pull” workout trains all of the upper body muscles that are involved in pulling exercises such as the back and biceps.
  • The “legs” workout trains the entire lower body which includes quads, hamstrings and calves.
